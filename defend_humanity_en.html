I had to choose a topic for my Matura paper in the year 2020. I already knew that I wanted to pursue something in computer science but I didn't know what exactly. Then I came upon the idea to develop an app, but not just any app. I decided to develop a video game for iOS.

If you click on the following link you will be redirected to the App Store. If you own an iPhone you can download the app and test it. Please keep in mind that I had almost no experience in the field of software or game development.

Defend Humanity

The controls are relatively easy. As soon as you started a round you will be able to stear your ship by touching the left side of the phone. If you tap on the right side, you will be able to shoot. The goal is to eliminate as much enemy ships as possible before your own ship is eliminated.
